# Engineering Journey

Working on Formula Bharat was one of the most valuable engineering experiences of my undergraduate studies. The project provided an opportunity to apply classroom concepts to the design and development of a real Formula Student race car.

As the Powertrain Lead, I experienced the complete engineering workflow—from initial planning and engineering calculations to manufacturing, assembly, subsystem integration, and testing. Every stage required balancing performance, reliability, manufacturability, and time constraints.

Serving as the Main Driver gave me an additional perspective on vehicle development. Testing the vehicle allowed me to understand how engineering decisions directly influenced vehicle performance and handling characteristics. Driver feedback became an important part of validating the design and identifying areas for improvement.

The project also strengthened my ability to work within multidisciplinary teams, communicate technical ideas effectively, and make engineering decisions under strict deadlines.

Overall, Formula Bharat significantly improved my practical engineering skills, leadership abilities, and confidence in solving real-world engineering problems.
