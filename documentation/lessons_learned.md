# Lessons Learned

Formula Bharat taught me lessons that extend far beyond technical knowledge.

## Engineering

- Practical engineering always involves balancing multiple design constraints.
- Manufacturing limitations play a significant role in design decisions.
- Small improvements can have a noticeable impact on overall system performance.

## Teamwork

- Effective communication between subsystem teams is essential.
- Good planning reduces manufacturing and assembly issues.
- Leadership requires supporting team members and making informed decisions.

## Testing

- Vehicle testing is essential for validating engineering assumptions.
- Driver feedback provides valuable insight for design refinement.
- Iterative testing helps improve vehicle reliability and performance.

## Personal Growth

This project improved my confidence in engineering problem solving, leadership, technical communication, and working under challenging project timelines. It reinforced the importance of continuous learning and practical experience in engineering.
