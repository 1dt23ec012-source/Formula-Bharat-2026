# Competition Overview

Formula Bharat is India's Formula Student competition that challenges engineering students to design, manufacture, assemble, and validate a formula-style race car.

The competition emphasizes technical excellence, innovation, teamwork, project management, and practical engineering skills. Teams are evaluated on engineering design, vehicle performance, cost, manufacturing, and overall project execution.

Participating in Formula Bharat provided hands-on experience in multidisciplinary engineering, allowing students to apply theoretical concepts to a real-world automotive project while working within industry-style engineering practices.

As the Powertrain Lead and Main Driver, I contributed to both the technical development and practical validation of the vehicle throughout the project.
